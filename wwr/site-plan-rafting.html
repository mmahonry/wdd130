Overview

Purpose

To present the thrilling world of river rafting adventures online and attract adventurers worldwide to experience our unique and exhilarating journeys while driving business growth.

Audience

>

Our website is designed for adventure enthusiasts and thrill-seekers of various ages and backgrounds. It provides essential information about our river rafting adventures, answers common questions, and facilitates easy booking. Visitors can access our site on desktops, laptops, or mobile devices.

>

Audience

Our target audience includes adventure enthusiasts, families seeking outdoor adventures, corporate groups looking for team-building experiences, and anyone with a passion for nature and excitement.

Scenario Questions:

  • Who are the target customers? - Our customers are adventure seekers of all ages.
  • What do they want? - They want thrilling rafting experiences and easy access to information about our trips.
  • What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? - They expect to find trip details, safety information, and booking options.

Branding

Website Logo

Our logo embodies the spirit of adventure, featuring a dynamic rafting scene that reflects the excitement our customers can expect.
